Online PECS binder

Hello and Welcome to the Online PECS Binder!

PECS
stands for Picture Exchange Communication System. The PECS helps anyone with communication challenges, such as persons with Autism, communicate through the exchange of pictures. The pictures which make up the vocublary of the individual are collected in a binder and the pictures are "exchanged"

An example of a PECS binder

PECS Phase IV
The Online Binder is designed for use in the 4th Phase of the PECS where an individual will use an “I want” picture followed by a picture of the item being requested.

Register for additional features!
If you register for an account you will be able to take advantage of a few additional features:
  • Upload and Edit personalized PECS
  • Add PECS to a personalized favorite tab

Quick Guide

    Strips
    Switch between I WANT or FIRST...THEN sentence structure by clicking/tapping on the strip tabs.
    Click/Tap on a PECS on the bottom section to complete the sentence structure, then
    click/tap the PECS on the strip, one at a time to play, or
    click/tap the play button to play the whole sentence Structure.
    Tabs
    Switch between the PECS categories by clicking/tapping on the tabs.
    Click/Tap on a PECS to populate and complete the Strip with the chosen PECS.
